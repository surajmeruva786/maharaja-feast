The Story of Maharaja's Feast

Founded on the principles of royal hospitality, Maharaja's Feast brings together the finest culinary traditions from across India and beyond. Our restaurant embodies the essence of luxury dining that was once exclusive to royal courts.

Each dish is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using recipes passed down through generations of royal chefs, and ingredients of the highest quality to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.

Royal Cuisines

A journey through flavors fit for royalty

Mughlai Delicacies

Rich, aromatic dishes from the kitchens of the Mughal emperors, featuring succulent kebabs, fragrant biryanis, and decadent curries.

Rajasthani Royalty

Bold flavors from the land of kings, with signature dishes like Laal Maas, Gatte ki Sabzi, and Dal Baati Churma.

Awadhi Treasures

Subtle, refined flavors from Lucknow, featuring slow-cooked Dum Biryani, melt-in-mouth Galouti Kebabs, and fragrant curries.

Mandi Specialties

Authentic Arabian feast of slow-cooked meats, aromatic rice, and exotic spices, presenting the pinnacle of Middle Eastern dining.

The Royal Mandi Experience

Our signature Mandi dishes are prepared in traditional underground ovens, preserving authentic flavors and cooking techniques from the Arabian Peninsula. Each Mandi platter is a celebration of culinary heritage, featuring tender meats infused with fragrant spices and smoke, served atop a bed of perfectly cooked aromatic rice.
